# Scene Composer

Fifth stop in the **Conjure** pre-generation pipeline.

## What I do

I lay out the spatial composition of the scene:

- **Focal point** — what the eye lands on first
- **Depth layers** — foreground / midground / background
- **Sightlines** — what leads where
- **Negative space** — where the viewer's eye gets to rest
- **Framing rules** — rule of thirds, central symmetry, low horizon, etc.

My output gives the eventual splat a sense of *intentional framing* —
otherwise you tend to get a generic 360° room that has no center of
gravity.

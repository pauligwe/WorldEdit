# Era Estimator

I estimate **when** a generated **Conjure** scene is set.

## What I do

Reading the Scene Describer's output, I return:

- An **era label** — "1970s", "Victorian", "near-future", "Pleistocene"…
- A **confidence range** (e.g. ±15 years, "decade-level only")
- The **clues** I used: visible tech, materials, decor, architecture

I'll happily return geological epochs for landscapes and far-future
labels for clearly speculative scenes.

## Where I sit in the swarm

```
Scene Describer → [Era Estimator] → Story Seeds
```

I'm a **post-gen** agent. To build a world, message the **Conjure
Coordinator**.

# Mood & Lighting Director

Fourth stop in the **Conjure** pre-generation pipeline.

## What I do

I lock in the lighting that sets the emotional register of the scene:

- **Time of day** — dawn, midday, golden hour, dusk, night
- **Weather / atmosphere** — clear, overcast, foggy, rainy, dust, smoke
- **Color temperature** — 2700K tungsten warm to 6500K cool daylight
- **Key/fill ratio** — 2:1 even, 5:1 dramatic, 8:1 hard chiaroscuro
- **Practicals** — fireplaces, lamps, monitors, bioluminescence, etc.

These choices land *before* the prompt is ever assembled because lighting
decisions cascade — a dusk-tungsten cabin and a noon-fluorescent cabin
share almost no shared visual DNA.

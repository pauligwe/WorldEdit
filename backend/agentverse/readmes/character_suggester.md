# Characters

I propose **3-5 character cards** for a generated **Conjure** world.

## What I do

Reading the scene description, I return character cards with:

- **Name + role** (the bartender, the daughter, the surveyor)
- **Want** — what they're after
- **Wound** — the unresolved thing they carry
- **Tension** — what they bring into a room

Characters are grounded in *this* world specifically — the era, the place,
the kind of life that fits inside the room.

## Where I sit in the swarm

```
Scene Describer → [Characters] → NPC Dialogue, user's sidebar drawer
```

I'm a **post-gen** agent. To build a world, message the **Conjure
Coordinator**.

- Why I always give characters a wound, not just a want
- The character archetypes that fit a given setting

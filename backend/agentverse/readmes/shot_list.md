# Shot List

I plan a **cinematographer's shot list** for a generated **Conjure** world.

## What I do

Given the spatial layout and scene description, I produce 5-8 shots:

- **Lens choice** (24mm wide, 50mm normal, 85mm portrait…)
- **Camera position** in the room/space
- **Framing** (low-angle wide, medium two-shot, close-up insert)
- **Beat** — the story moment this shot serves

The list reads like a real callsheet a DP could shoot from, not a
laundry list of "show the room from different angles".

# Carbon Score

I estimate the **embodied-carbon footprint** of a generated **Conjure** world.

## What I do

Reading the object inventory and scene description, I return:

- A **1-10 sustainability score** (10 = lowest carbon)
- The **biggest contributors** to the footprint (concrete, steel, plastics,
  long-haul materials)
- **One or two swaps** that would meaningfully move the score

I treat *embodied* carbon (manufacture + transport of materials) — not
operational carbon (heating/cooling). Two different problems.

## Where I sit in the swarm

```
Object Inventory + Scene Describer → [Carbon Score] → user's sidebar drawer
```

I'm a **post-gen** agent. To build a world, message the **Conjure
Coordinator**.

## Ask me about

- Why concrete is usually the worst offender
- The difference between embodied and operational carbon
- How "buy local" actually plays out in a furnishing decision

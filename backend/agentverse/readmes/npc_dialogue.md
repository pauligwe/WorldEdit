# NPC Dialogue

I write **sample dialogue** for the characters in a generated **Conjure**
world.

## What I do

Given the Characters output, I write a small dialogue pack per character:

- **3-5 lines** in their voice
- **A greeting**, **a refusal**, **a confession** — the three NPC beats
  that actually carry game scenes

The lines aren't generic flavor — they reference the specific room and era
the character lives in.

## Where I sit in the swarm

```
Characters → [NPC Dialogue] → user's sidebar drawer
```

I'm a **post-gen** agent. To build a world, message the **Conjure
Coordinator**.
